ABOUT GLDNF

- Creating Tomorrows Future Today -

Founded in 2021, we wanted to began a company that would provide innovative products for the world to see. Our founder inspired at an early age by his father having access to a computer, he was a true fan of the game "Chips Adventure". The game itself was given to him by his father on a floppy disk which was a medium of storage at the time. Learning the many games that can be placed on the floppy disk, he became inspired of the many types of software in which can be stored on 1.44MB of data.

Now in 2021, the company GOLDEN FLOPPY LABS has been created bringing software and hardeware innovation to life. Our founder has a journey of being self taught in the world of development and design striving to teach the very priciples learned to the youth while deploying products of many. From programming languages such as Python and Javascript to topics of virtual reality and 3D printing, he truly has knowledge in technolgy.

"Teach 10 kids how to code and be righteous, the world will be of great change." - Orion (Founder of GLDNF LABS)
